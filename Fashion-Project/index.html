Let your wardrobe tell your story




Are you always struggling with your appearance? That's probably because you're doing it wrong. Don't worry, all that is about to change. But first, ditch those ill-fitting store bought cloths then let's take you through a fun and exciting journey of creating and designing a wardrobe that'll make getting dressed no longer a nightmare.

With our resources you can be sure of transitioning from store bought clothing to a hundred percent handmade clothing in no time.

What's more? Our resources cuts across all sewing levels and completion times. So you're a novice sewer or you've got a tight schedule? Relax! We got you!


Want to learn more? Keep scrolling.

How it works


Inspiration


Our inspiration resources will show you how to collect inspirations from sources like the internet and magazines and organize them into themes that your future project will be based on


Body Shape


You'll learn how to use "My Body Model" which takes your precise body shape and drafts your inspiration on it so you'll see what the finished garment looks like on you before starting to make it


Color Palette


Learn to use "Colorsnap" - an app which takes your eyes, hair, and skin tone to determine over fifty color palettes that are just right for you - or you can get a session with our professional color consultants


Fabric


From silks to brocades, you'll learn how to use different fabrics to achieve differnt looks with the same pattern. And also learn how to make prints from floral to whatever you like right from the comfort of your home using a scanner


Patterns & Hacks


Aside from the hundreds of free and paid patterns on our site. You'll also get access to free hack tutorials and one-on-one sessions with our professionals to transform those ill-fitting store bought cloths into wardrobe staples. The inspirations that you really like but didn't make cut can be hacked to appeal to your body shape


Project Resources


Our project tools will help you prioritize and organize your projects into seasonal or yearly queues. We've got reminders to keep you focused so that you meet your goals. We've also got a large community of makers just like you who are ready to help when the heat is on
